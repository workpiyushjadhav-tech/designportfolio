About Me

Designer. Researcher.
Former social worker.

Before picking up Figma, I spent nearly a decade working with people in crisis — listening, problem-solving, and building trust under pressure. That background shapes every design decision I make.

I don't just design screens. I design for the human on the other side — their frustrations, their contexts, and what they actually need to succeed.

Product Design UX Research Prototyping Design Systems Figma User Testing Accessibility Visual Design
More about me
01 — Approach
People First, Always
Research isn't a phase — it's a practice. I interview, observe, and test continuously to make sure design decisions are grounded in real behavior, not assumptions.
02 — Process
Think, Make, Test, Repeat
I move fast in low fidelity and slow down where it matters. Prototypes get validated early, ideas get killed early, and the best solutions emerge from iteration — not perfection.
03 — Craft
Pixel-level Intentionality
Good UX is invisible. Great visual design amplifies it. I care about the 2px that separates "polished" from "almost there."
